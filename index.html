About Me

Jessica completed her PhD in quantum physics at the Australian National University in 2020, with a thesis titled "The emergence of chaos in continuously monitored open quantum systems". She worked as a research associate in Non Hermitian and PT Symmetric quantum chaos at Imperial College London for four years.
